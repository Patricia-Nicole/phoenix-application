University


Your way




Are you searching for a good university? You don't know what is your way?

Don't worry!
I have some advices for you! Make sure you choose the university because you want to, and not because your parents want that for you or you will be there with your friends.


It is your future, so focus on choosing something that is making you happy now and it will make you happy in future as well.
Maybe you don't know how to search for a good university?
I was there too. All you need to do is to make a plan.

I will show you what I did.


Make an idea of the universities in the whole world.


Once you found an intersting university, you can search for the univesrity's requirements. I had a look over the universities in Netherlands and UK.


In UK you need an account on UCAS. There you can select maximum of 5 universities from UK. From those 5 you need to select 2 afterwards as your first and second choice. If you want, I can give you more detais about the procedure on email.


In Netherlands you need an account on Studielink. Usually in Netherlands you need to take one of their universities exams and afterwards you will see if you are accepted or not. Don't worry, usually they give you all the materials you need to study before the exam.


How to search for a good couse?
I will show you a good way to find something that you like.

First thing is to search the official website of the university.



Secondly you search for undergraduate courses.



Afterwards you start selecting the departments you are interested in. For UK the UCAS code on the left is really important to keep in mind.



Take a good look at the following:

Entry Requirements
Course description
Career opportunities
Fees
Scholarships/sponsorships opportunities

And if you are not sure that the details on the website are enough for you to take a decision, then email them or call them using the Contact Details on the website.


If you have questions, don't hesitate to ask!
